Why Open Source Software?

    Published: Aug 15, 2018 : OSS
These days, free and open source software (FOSS) is in vogue across many fields, both in academia and in industry, though there are holdouts, and it’s not always the path of least resistance. I’m not here to evangelize for open source, and I’m not a purist: closed-source and proprietary work has its place in our society. However, when it comes to research, it seems to me that open source should be the default, for the libraries and products we make, as well as the tools we use to make them.
