References

Many texts in applied mathematics and other applied sciences use MATLAB as their "pseudocode" of choice since it is easy to write algorithms in with a minimum of extra baggage. The following are some texts I have looked at over the years to get some new ideas about MATLAB. First and foremost, your best guide is the documentation itself.

  • D. J. Higham and N. J. Higham. MATLAB Guide. Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics, 2nd Edition, 2005.
  • R. K. Johnson. Elements of MATLAB Style. Cambridge University Press, 2011.
  • W. Young Yang, et. al. Applied Numerical Methods using MATLAB. Wiley-Interscience, 2005.
